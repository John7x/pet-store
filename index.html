John's Pet Store Guide

Dogs

Dogs can be the most loyal comapnions you can have!

Cats

Cats are pretty much the most popular pets you can have. Make sure you feed them on a daily basis.

Rabbits

Cutest of the bunch. But make sure as an owner, you pick the right food to feed them and put them in a cage.
Guinea Pigs
Probably the most difficult pet to take care of. Make sure to clean their cage once a week and watch out for any illness they carry.
Betta Fishes
These fishes need to be carefeul as well. Do not overfeed them. Baby bettas should only eat smaller flakes.
